Background

Mr. K.K., EDM Music Producer, mainly creating Future Bass & Melodic Dubstep music got a burn out in early 2022 moving him completely away from music (production). During this, listening to a lot of J-pop & similar genre of music, a voice in his head always encouraged him to just do it... just make music again and sometimes teasing with "if you can't make music again, you should die instead". On August 6, 2022; he started "I'm alive, I'll be alive" with no intention to release it at all. With influence came from listening to such music, it was something different from what he used to make. Giving that voice a name, he released "I'm Alive" on November 12, 2022 under an alias notTaki.
